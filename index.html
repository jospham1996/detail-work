1.2. User Personas

Anna Nguyen
Marketing Manager
Age: 40
Nationality: Vietnamese
Individual Travelers
Context
Anna is a busy professional planning a short vacation to Thailand. She has little experience with visa applications and wants a fast, hassle-free process without reading complex legal requirements. She prefers using mobile devices for applications and needs clear guidance.
Goal
Complete the visa application quickly and easily.
Avoid complicated forms and unclear instructions.
Pain Points
Confused about required documents.
Frustrated by long and complex forms.
Difficulties in tracking application progress.
David Lee
College Student
Age: 24
Nationality: Korean
Visa Education
Context
David is traveling abroad for an exchange program and applying for a visa for the first time. He is unfamiliar with visa processes and finds the terminology confusing. He is afraid of making mistakes that could delay or reject his application, and his parents are also unsure about the requirements.
Goal
Receive clear, easy-to-understand instructions.
Ensure his application is correct before submission.
Pain Points
Overwhelmed by unclear requirements.
Unsure if his documents meet the criteria.
Confused by technical visa terms.
Arjun Sharma
Business Owner
Age: 32
Nationality: Indian
Urgent Visa Applicant
Context
Sharma has just been assigned to attend a high-stakes business meeting in Dubai next week. He needs to get his visa approved as quickly as possible and is willing to pay extra for expedited processing. He has no time for long forms or unclear processing times.
Goal
Get a visa approval in the shortest time possible.
Avoid delays and unnecessary steps.
Pain Points
Unclear visa processing timelines.
Frustrated by lengthy verification processes.
Wants a fast-track option but doesn't see one easily.
Sarah Wong
